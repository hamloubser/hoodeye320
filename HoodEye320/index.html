About this App

Revolutionary crime-reporting, safety and security application


Hoodeye is a trusted tool for crime prevention and personal security and is designed to keep you and your family safe, should a threat arise. Hoodeye will give you and your loved ones peace of mind and a sense of security knowing that you have a discrete method of reporting crimes with the ability to capture GPS embedded images, video, audio, and custom descriptions. In an emergency situation, a customizable "Panic button" allows Hoodeye users the ability to send multiple, GPS-tagged distress messages to a list of emergency contacts and family members with the single push of a button. Additional features include a “Safety box” loaded with essential safety tools like a flashlight, and emergency contact numbers. We believe that safety and protection should always be close at hand and by utilizing the latest in mobile phone technology, Hoodeye makes it possible to achieve safety and security for You and your family as discreetly and intelligently as possible.
